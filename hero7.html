Franco

Rank: Top 7 Roam Hero

Franco is a tank hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, famous for his hook and crowd control abilities. He can pull enemies into his team, initiate fights, and disrupt the enemy formation effectively.

His durability allows him to absorb damage while controlling enemies with his hook and ultimate. Franco excels in coordinated play, setting up kills for his team and protecting allies in high-pressure situations.
