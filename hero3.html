Khufra

Rank: Top 3 Roam Hero

Khufra is a tank hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, famous for his crowd control and initiator skills. He excels at diving into enemy formations to disrupt and disable multiple opponents, creating opportunities for his team.

His abilities allow him to knock up, slow, and immobilize enemies while absorbing massive damage. Khufra is especially powerful against high-mobility heroes, preventing them from escaping or executing their combos effectively.
