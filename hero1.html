Mathilda

Rank: Top 1 Roam Hero

Mathilda is a support/mobility hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, known for her ability to assist allies with powerful shields and mobility boosts. She combines speed and control, making her one of the most reliable roamers in competitive gameplay.

Using her skills, Mathilda can dash around the battlefield, protect teammates, and engage enemies efficiently. She is especially strong when paired with high-damage heroes, helping them secure kills while staying safe herself.
