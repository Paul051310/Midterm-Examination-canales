Grock

Rank: Top 2 Roam Hero

Grock is a tank hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, known for his high durability and ability to control the battlefield. His skills allow him to block enemy movement, initiate team fights, and absorb huge amounts of damage for his allies.

Grock excels at disrupting enemy formations and protecting squishier teammates. His combination of mobility and crowd control makes him one of the strongest frontline heroes in competitive play.
