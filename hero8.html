Belerick

Rank: Top 8 Roam Hero

Belerick is a tank/support hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, known for his high durability and crowd control abilities. He can absorb large amounts of damage while slowing and controlling enemies with his skills.

Belerick excels at protecting teammates, disrupting enemy formations, and turning the tide of team fights. His passive and ultimate make him a resilient frontline hero, ideal for both solo and coordinated gameplay.
