Arlott

Rank: Top 9 Roam Hero

Arlott is a tank/fighter hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, known for his strong initiation and crowd control abilities. He can engage multiple enemies, absorb damage, and disrupt enemy formations while protecting his team.

Arlott excels at starting fights, controlling the battlefield, and providing frontline support. His combination of durability and impactful skills makes him a reliable hero in both casual and competitive play.
