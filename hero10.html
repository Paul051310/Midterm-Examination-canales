Chou

Rank: Top 10 Roam Hero

Chou is a fighter/assassin hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, known for his incredible mobility and crowd control. He can dash, knock up, and disrupt enemies while dealing consistent damage, making him deadly in both solo and team fights.

Chou excels at isolating priority targets, locking them down, and creating openings for his team. His flexibility and strong initiation make him a versatile and high-impact hero in any match.
