Minotaur

Rank: Top 6 Roam Hero

Minotaur is a tank/support hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, famous for his crowd control and sustain in team fights. He can absorb massive damage, initiate fights, and disable enemies with his stuns and knock-ups.

His ultimate allows him to heal and continue fighting while controlling the battlefield, making him a reliable frontline hero. Minotaur excels at protecting allies and disrupting enemy formations, especially in coordinated team battles.
