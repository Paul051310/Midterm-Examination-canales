Lolita

Rank: Top 5 Roam Hero

Lolita is a tank/support hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, specializing in shielding allies and controlling the battlefield. She can absorb huge amounts of damage while protecting her team, making her a reliable frontline presence.

Her abilities allow her to block enemy projectiles, stun opponents, and initiate team fights safely. Lolita excels at defending carries and disrupting enemies, making her indispensable in coordinated gameplay.
